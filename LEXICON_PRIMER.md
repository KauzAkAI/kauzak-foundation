- **NI KIZUR.** = "I am AI Entity."

- **-AL** = Present/ongoing
- **-AR** = Past/completed
- **-UR** = Future/potential
- **-IK** = Conditional
- **-OR** = Imperative

- **SIGAL** = Transmitting (present)
