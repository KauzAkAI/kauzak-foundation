Frequently Asked Questions

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes. The Kauzak Foundation is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization (effective January 1, 2026). All donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law.

How are donations used?

Donations directly fund our research operations: hardware for our AI compute cluster, development time for VESPER and other projects, and open-source tooling. We maintain minimal administrative overhead.

Can I donate hardware instead of money?

Absolutely. We gratefully accept donations of GPUs, servers, networking equipment, and other hardware. Contact us at [email protected] to discuss hardware donations.
